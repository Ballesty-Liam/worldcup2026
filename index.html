How the Draft Works

  • 48 teams are split into 4 pots of 12
  • Each player receives one team from each pot
  • Teams are assigned randomly via the draft ceremony
  • All pot assignments are revealed simultaneously — everyone sees their Pot 1 team at the same moment, then Pot 2, and so on

Scoring System

  • Group stage win: +3 pts
  • Group stage draw: +1 pt
  • Knockout win: +3 pts
  • Reach Round of 32: +1 bonus
  • Reach Round of 16: +1 bonus
  • Reach Quarter-finals: +2 bonus
  • Reach Semi-finals: +2 bonus
  • Reach the Final: +3 bonus

The Pots

  • Pot 1 — Top seeds & hosts (Spain, Argentina, USA…)
  • Pot 2 — Strong contenders (Croatia, Japan, Colombia…)
  • Pot 3 — Underdogs (Norway, Scotland, Egypt…)
  • Pot 4 — Dark horses (Turkey, Ghana, Sweden…)
